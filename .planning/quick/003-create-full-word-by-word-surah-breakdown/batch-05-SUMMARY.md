## Grammar Highlights by Surah

### 090 - Al-Balad (The City)

**Key Constructs:**
- **Emphatic oath formula:** لَا أُقْسِمُ (la uqsimu) - negation used for emphasis
- **Dual forms:** عَيْنَيْنِ, شَفَتَيْنِ, النَّجْدَيْنِ demonstrating dual accusative endings
- **The five nouns (الأسماء الخمسة):** ذِي (genitive), ذَا (accusative) showing case by long vowels
- **Rhetorical questions:** أَيَحْسَبُ, أَلَمْ نَجْعَل
- **Form VI verbs:** تَوَاصَوْا (mutual/reciprocal action)
- **Form VIII verbs:** اقْتَحَمَ (intensive action)
- **Idafa constructions:** فَكُّ رَقَبَةٍ, أَصْحَابُ الْمَيْمَنَةِ

### 091 - Ash-Shams (The Sun)

**Key Constructs:**
- **Extended oath series:** One of the longest in the Quran - 7 consecutive وَ oaths (verses 1-7)
- **Form II verbs (intensive):** جَلَّاهَا, سَوَّاهَا, زَكَّاهَا, دَسَّاهَا, كَذَّبَتْ
- **Form IV verbs:** أَلْهَمَهَا (inspiring with dual object), أَفْلَحَ (success)
- **Form VII verb:** انبَعَثَ (reflexive/passive meaning)
- **Quadriliteral verb:** دَمْدَمَ (د-م-د-م) for emphatic destruction
- **Superlative form:** أَشْقَاهَا (أَفْعَل pattern for "most wretched")
- **Perfect parallelism:** قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّاهَا vs. قَدْ خَابَ مَن دَسَّاهَا
- **Temporal إِذَا clauses:** Verses 2, 3, 4 qualifying the oaths

### 092 - Al-Layl (The Night)

**Key Constructs:**
- **أَمَّا...فَ conditional construction:** Perfect structural parallelism between verses 5-7 (righteous) and 8-10 (wicked)
- **Comparative superlatives:** الْيُسْرَىٰ vs. الْعُسْرَىٰ, الْأَشْقَى vs. الْأَتْقَى, الْحُسْنَىٰ, الْأُولَىٰ
- **Form V verbs (reflexive):** تَجَلَّىٰ, تَرَدَّىٰ, تَوَلَّىٰ, تَلَظَّىٰ, يَتَزَكَّىٰ
- **Form X verb:** اسْتَغْنَىٰ (considering oneself to possess a quality)
- **Future tense particles:** سَ (will) and سَوْفَ (emphatic future)
- **Multiple emphasis layers:** إِنَّ...لَ, وَلَسَوْفَ (triple emphasis)
- **Passive constructions:** يُجَنَّبُهَا (Form II passive), تُجْزَىٰ
- **Exception structures:** لَا...إِلَّا (none except)
