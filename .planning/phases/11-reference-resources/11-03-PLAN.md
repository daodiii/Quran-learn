## Form I: The Base Pattern (الفِعْل الأَوَّل)

**Pattern:** فَعَلَ (faʿala)

Form I is the simplest, most common verb pattern. All other forms are derived from it. It has no added letters, just the three root consonants with vowels.

**Common meanings:** Basic action, no modification

### Past Tense (المَاضِي)

**Pattern notes:**
- Past tense adds suffixes to the root: تُ، تَ، تِ، نَا، etc.
- 3rd person masculine singular has no suffix (just vowels)
- Dual forms use same suffix for both genders

### Present Tense (المُضَارِع)

<VerbConjugation

    ["2nd fem. (you)", "تَكْتُبِينَ (taktubīna)", "تَكْتُبَانِ (taktubāni)", "تَكْتُبْنَ (taktubna)"],
    ["3rd masc. (he/they)", "يَكْتُبُ (yaktubu)", "يَكْتُبَانِ (yaktubāni)", "يَكْتُبُونَ (yaktubūna)"],
    ["3rd fem. (she/they)", "تَكْتُبُ (taktubu)", "تَكْتُبَانِ (taktubāni)", "يَكْتُبْنَ (yaktubna)"]
  ]}
/>

**Pattern notes:**
- Present uses prefixes: أ، ت، ي، ن
- Middle vowel varies by verb (here: u - this is the يَفْعُلُ pattern)
- Dual and plural add suffixes

- Imperative only has 2nd person (commanding someone)

**Forms to Include (per RSRC-02 and TERMINOLOGY.md):**

1. **Form I (فَعَلَ)** - Base pattern, most common
   - Past: كَتَبَ (kataba)
   - Present: يَكْتُبُ (yaktubu)
   - Imperative: اُكْتُبْ (uktub)
   - Meaning: Basic action

2. **Form II (فَعَّلَ)** - Doubled middle letter
   - Past: عَلَّمَ (ʿallama)
   - Present: يُعَلِّمُ (yuʿallimu)
   - Meaning: Intensive/causative action

3. **Form III (فَاعَلَ)** - Alif after first root letter
   - Past: جَاهَدَ (jāhada)
   - Present: يُجَاهِدُ (yujāhidu)
   - Meaning: To do with/toward someone

4. **Form IV (أَفْعَلَ)** - Hamza prefix
   - Past: أَنْزَلَ (anzala)
   - Present: يُنْزِلُ (yunzilu)
   - Meaning: Causative

5. **Form V (تَفَعَّلَ)** - Ta prefix + doubled middle
   - Past: تَعَلَّمَ (taʿallama)
   - Present: يَتَعَلَّمُ (yataʿallamu)
   - Meaning: Reflexive of Form II

6. **Form VI (تَفَاعَلَ)** - Ta prefix + alif
   - Past: تَبَارَكَ (tabāraka)
   - Present: يَتَبَارَكُ (yatabāraku)
   - Meaning: Reflexive of Form III

7. **Form VII (اِنْفَعَلَ)** - In prefix
   - Past: اِنْفَطَرَ (infaṭara)
   - Present: يَنْفَطِرُ (yanfaṭiru)
   - Meaning: Passive/reflexive

8. **Form VIII (اِفْتَعَلَ)** - Infixed ta
   - Past: اِسْتَمَعَ (istamaʿa)
   - Present: يَسْتَمِعُ (yastamiʿu)
   - Meaning: Reflexive action

9. **Form IX (اِفْعَلَّ)** - Doubled last letter (rare, colors/defects)
   - Past: اِحْمَرَّ (iḥmarra)
   - Present: يَحْمَرُّ (yaḥmarru)
   - Meaning: To become [color/quality]

10. **Form X (اِسْتَفْعَلَ)** - Ista prefix
    - Past: اِسْتَغْفَرَ (istaghfara)
    - Present: يَسْتَغْفِرُ (yastaghfiru)
