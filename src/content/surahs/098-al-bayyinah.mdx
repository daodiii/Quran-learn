# Al-Bayyinah (The Clear Evidence)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Medinan
- **Verses:** 8
- **Theme:** The arrival of the Prophet Muhammad as clear proof (bayyinah) to People of the Book and polytheists. Describes the pure religion's essence and the ultimate fate of believers and disbelievers in the Hereafter.
- **Grammar Focus:** Lam yakun negation, relative clauses, active participles (ism fa'il), superlative constructions, nominal sentences, dual number, emphasis through repetition

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** لَمْ يَكُنِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِنْ أَهْلِ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ وَٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ مُنفَكِّينَ حَتَّىٰ تَأْتِيَهُمُ ٱلْبَيِّنَةُ

**Translation:** "Those who disbelieved from the People of the Book and the polytheists were not to desist until there came to them the clear evidence"

- Al-bayyinah refers to Prophet Muhammad and the Quran

## Grammar Summary

### Main Grammatical Constructs

1. **Lam yakun negation**: Verse 1 uses lam with defective verb kāna in jussive mood

2. **Relative clauses**: Alladhīna constructions throughout (verses 1, 4, 6, 7)

3. **Active participles (ism fa'il)**:
   - Munfakkīna (desisting)
   - Mukhlisīna (sincere ones)
   - Khālidīna (abiding ones)
   - Al-mushrikīna (polytheists)

4. **Passive participles (ism maf'ul)**: Mutahharah (purified)

5. **Badal (apposition)**: Verse 2 rasūlun as badal to al-bayyinah

6. **Nominal sentences with prepositional phrases**: Verse 3 (fīhā kutubun), verse 8 ('inda rabbihim)

7. **Passive voice**: Ūtū, umirū (were given, were commanded)

8. **Ma... illā construction**: Verses 4-5 for restriction/exception

9. **Purpose clauses with li-**: Verse 5 (li-ya'budū) with subjunctive mood

10. **Hal (circumstantial) clauses**: Mukhlisīna, hunafā', khālidīna showing states

11. **Inna emphasis constructions**: Verses 6-7 for strong assertion

12. **Superlative constructions in idafa**: Sharru al-bariyyah, khayru al-bariyyah

13. **Fasl pronoun (hum)**: Verses 6-7 for emphasis between mubtada' and khabar

14. **Coordinated subjunctive verbs**: Verse 5 (ya'budū, yuqīmū, yu'tū)

15. **Mutual action**: Verse 8 radiya Allāhu 'anhum wa-radū 'anhu

### Pedagogical Notes

This surah is excellent for teaching:
- The lam yakun construction (negated past continuous)
- Contrast structures (believers vs. disbelievers, best vs. worst)
- Active and passive participles as adjectives and nouns
- Purpose clauses with subjunctive mood
- The badal (appositive) construction
- Superlatives in idafa construction
- Hal (circumstantial state) clauses with active participles
- The ma... illā restrictive construction
- Fasl pronouns for emphasis in nominal sentences
