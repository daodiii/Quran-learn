# Al-Fil (The Elephant)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 5
- **Theme:** Recounts how Allah protected the Ka'bah from Abraha's elephant army in the Year of the Elephant, demonstrating divine power and protection of sacred places.
- **Grammar Focus:** Interrogative أَلَمْ (alam) with past tense, past tense narrative verbs, accusative objects, prepositional phrases, attached pronouns, relative clauses

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَابِ الْفِيلِ

**Translation:** "Have you not seen how your Lord dealt with the companions of the elephant?"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | أَلَمْ | alam | - | Particle - interrogative + negation | Interrogative particle with negation - not declinable | Did not? Have not? |
| 2 | تَرَ | tara | ر ا ي | Verb - Form I, present tense (jussive), 2nd person, masculine, singular | Jussive verb (fi'l mudari' majzum) after لَمْ - jussive (majzum) | you see |
| 3 | كَيْفَ | kayfa | - | Adverb - interrogative | Interrogative adverb - not declinable | how |
| 4 | فَعَلَ | fa'ala | ف ع ل | Verb - Form I, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | did, dealt |
| 5 | رَبُّكَ | rabbuka | ر ب ب | Noun - masculine, singular, definite + attached pronoun | Subject (fa'il) - nominative (marfu') with damma, + possessive pronoun genitive | your Lord |
| 6 | بِ | bi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | with, to |
| 7 | أَصْحَابِ | ashabi | ص ح ب | Noun - masculine, plural, definite (by idafa) | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | companions, people |
| 8 | الْفِيلِ | al-fil | ف ي ل | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with kasra | the elephant |

#### Grammar Notes
- أَلَمْ is a combination of hamzat al-istifham (أَ) and the negation/jussive particle لَمْ
- لَمْ causes the following present tense verb to be in jussive mood (majzum)
- تَرَ is shortened from تَرَى (the alif is dropped in jussive)
- كَيْفَ introduces an indirect question (how)
- أَصْحَابِ الْفِيلِ is an idafa construction meaning "the companions/people of the elephant"

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** أَلَمْ يَجْعَلْ كَيْدَهُمْ فِي تَضْلِيلٍ

**Translation:** "Did He not make their plan into misguidance?"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | أَلَمْ | alam | - | Particle - interrogative + negation | Interrogative particle with negation - not declinable | Did not? |
| 2 | يَجْعَلْ | yaj'al | ج ع ل | Verb - Form I, present tense (jussive), 3rd person, masculine, singular | Jussive verb (fi'l mudari' majzum) after لَمْ - jussive with sukun | make, render |
| 3 | كَيْدَهُمْ | kaydahum | ك ي د | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite + attached pronoun | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with fatha, + possessive pronoun genitive | their plot, scheme |
| 4 | فِي | fi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | in, into |
| 5 | تَضْلِيلٍ | tadlilin | ض ل ل | Noun - verbal noun (masdar), masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | misguidance, error, going astray |

#### Grammar Notes
- يَجْعَلْ is jussive (majzum) due to لَمْ, shown by sukun on the final letter
- جَعَلَ means "to make/render something into something"
- كَيْدَهُمْ: the pronoun هُمْ (their) is attached to the noun
- فِي تَضْلِيلٍ is the second object/complement showing what the plot became
- تَضْلِيلٍ is a masdar (verbal noun) from Form II verb ضَلَّلَ

### Verse 3

**Arabic:** وَأَرْسَلَ عَلَيْهِمْ طَيْرًا أَبَابِيلَ

**Translation:** "And He sent against them birds in flocks"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 2 | أَرْسَلَ | arsala | ر س ل | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | sent |
| 3 | عَلَيْهِمْ | 'alayhim | - | Preposition + attached pronoun | Preposition + pronoun genitive | upon them, against them |
| 4 | طَيْرًا | tayran | ط ي ر | Noun - masculine, collective/singular, indefinite | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with tanwin fatha | birds |
| 5 | أَبَابِيلَ | ababila | - | Noun - plural, indefinite | Adjective/state (hal) describing tayran - accusative (mansub) | in flocks, in groups |

#### Grammar Notes
- أَرْسَلَ is Form IV (causative: "to send forth")
- عَلَيْهِمْ: preposition عَلَى + attached pronoun هِمْ (them)
- طَيْرًا is a collective noun (can refer to singular or plural birds)
- أَبَابِيلَ is a plural form describing the birds as coming in successive flocks
- أَبَابِيلَ is in accusative (mansub) as a hal (state/circumstance) describing how the birds came

### Verse 4

**Arabic:** تَرْمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍ مِّن سِجِّيلٍ

**Translation:** "Striking them with stones of baked clay"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | تَرْمِيهِم | tarmihim | ر م ي | Verb - Form I, present tense, 3rd person, feminine, singular + attached pronoun | Present verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu'), + object pronoun accusative | strikes them, pelts them |
| 2 | بِ | bi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | with, by |
| 3 | حِجَارَةٍ | hijaratin | ح ج ر | Noun - feminine, singular (collective), indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | stones |
| 4 | مِّن | min | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | from, of |
| 5 | سِجِّيلٍ | sijjilin | - | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | baked clay, hardened clay |

#### Grammar Notes
- تَرْمِيهِم: present tense verb (describing ongoing action) + attached object pronoun هِم
- The verb is feminine singular agreeing with طَيْرًا (birds - collective noun treated as feminine)
- حِجَارَةٍ is also a collective noun meaning "stones"
- مِّن سِجِّيلٍ describes the material/source of the stones
- سِجِّيلٍ may be a loanword or Arabic term for hardened clay used as projectiles

### Verse 5

**Arabic:** فَجَعَلَهُمْ كَعَصْفٍ مَّأْكُولٍ

**Translation:** "And He made them like eaten straw"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | فَ | fa | - | Particle - conjunction | Consequential conjunction - not declinable | so, then |
| 2 | جَعَلَهُمْ | ja'alahum | ج ع ل | Verb - Form I, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) + object pronoun accusative | made them |
| 3 | كَ | ka | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition of comparison - not declinable | like, as |
| 4 | عَصْفٍ | 'asfin | ع ص ف | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | straw, chaff |
| 5 | مَّأْكُولٍ | ma'kulin | ا ك ل | Noun - passive participle, masculine, singular, indefinite | Adjective describing 'asfin - genitive with tanwin kasra | eaten, devoured |

#### Grammar Notes
- فَ indicates consequence/result (so, then)
- جَعَلَ here means "to make/render" (causative)
- جَعَلَهُمْ: verb + attached object pronoun هُمْ (them) in accusative
- كَ is a preposition meaning "like" used for comparison
- مَّأْكُولٍ is a passive participle (ism maf'ul) from أَكَلَ (to eat)
- The image: the army was destroyed like straw that has been eaten/devoured by animals

## Grammar Summary
- **Interrogative أَلَمْ construction**: Combines question + negation with rhetorical effect
- **Jussive mood**: لَمْ causes present tense verbs to take jussive (majzum) mood
- **Idafa constructions**: أَصْحَابِ الْفِيلِ, رَبُّكَ showing possessive relationships
- **Attached pronouns**: ـكَ (your), ـهُمْ (their/them) showing possession and objects
- **Past tense narrative**: فَعَلَ, أَرْسَلَ, جَعَلَ creating sequential story
- **Present tense for vividness**: تَرْمِي brings the action to life in the present
- **Prepositional phrases**: بِأَصْحَابِ, فِي تَضْلِيلٍ, عَلَيْهِمْ, بِحِجَارَةٍ, مِّن سِجِّيلٍ
- **Form IV verbs**: أَرْسَلَ (causative: to send forth)
- **Passive participle**: مَّأْكُولٍ (eaten) describing the final state
- **Comparison with كَ**: كَعَصْفٍ مَّأْكُولٍ (like eaten straw)
- **Consequential فَ**: فَجَعَلَهُمْ showing result
- **Hal (circumstantial state)**: أَبَابِيلَ describing the manner of the birds' coming
