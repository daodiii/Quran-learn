# Al-ʿAsr (Time)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 3
- **Theme:** The surah emphasizes that all of humanity is in loss except those who believe, do righteous deeds, and encourage one another to truth and patience.
- **Grammar Focus:** Oath construction with وَ (wa), nominal sentence structure, exceptive particle إِلَّا (illa), relative clauses with الَّذِينَ (alladhina)

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** وَالْعَصْرِ

**Translation:** "By time"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - oath prefix | Oath particle (harf al-qasam) - not declinable (mabni) | By |
| 2 | الْعَصْرِ | al-'asr | ع ص ر | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Object of oath (muqsam bihi) - genitive (majrur) with kasra | time, afternoon |

#### Grammar Notes
- The وَ (wa) is an oath particle specifically used for swearing by something
- الْعَصْرِ is in the genitive case because oath particles govern the genitive
- This verse establishes the subject being sworn by before stating what is sworn to

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** إِنَّ الْإِنسَانَ لَفِي خُسْرٍ

**Translation:** "Indeed, mankind is in loss"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | إِنَّ | inna | - | Particle - emphasis | Emphasis particle (harf tawkid) - governs accusative | indeed |
| 2 | الْإِنسَانَ | al-insana | ا ن س | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Subject of inna (ism inna) - accusative (mansub) with fatha | mankind, humanity |
| 3 | لَ | la | - | Particle - emphasis | Emphasis prefix particle (lam al-tawkid) - not declinable | surely |
| 4 | فِي | fi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable (mabni) | in |
| 5 | خُسْرٍ | khusrin | خ س ر | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | loss |

#### Grammar Notes
- إِنَّ is one of the particles that makes the subject accusative (nawasikh)
- The لَ prefix before فِي adds extra emphasis (lam al-tawkid)
- The predicate (khabar inna) is the prepositional phrase فِي خُسْرٍ
- خُسْرٍ is indefinite (no alif-lam) showing general/absolute loss

### Verse 3

**Arabic:** إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ

**Translation:** "Except for those who have believed and done righteous deeds and advised each other to truth and advised each other to patience"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | إِلَّا | illa | - | Particle - exception | Exception particle (harf istithna) - not declinable | except |
| 2 | الَّذِينَ | alladhina | - | Pronoun - relative, plural masculine | Excepted noun (mustathna) - accusative (mansub) position | those who |
| 3 | آمَنُوا | amanu | ا م ن | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | believed |
| 4 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 5 | عَمِلُوا | 'amilu | ع م ل | Verb - Form I, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | did, worked |
| 6 | الصَّالِحَاتِ | as-salihat | ص ل ح | Noun - feminine, plural, definite | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with kasra (sound feminine plural ending) | righteous deeds |
| 7 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 8 | تَوَاصَوْا | tawasaw | و ص ي | Verb - Form VI, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | mutually counseled |
| 9 | بِ | bi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | with, regarding |
| 10 | الْحَقِّ | al-haqq | ح ق ق | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | the truth |
| 11 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 12 | تَوَاصَوْا | tawasaw | و ص ي | Verb - Form VI, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | mutually counseled |
| 13 | بِ | bi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | with, regarding |
| 14 | الصَّبْرِ | as-sabr | ص ب ر | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | patience |

#### Grammar Notes
- إِلَّا introduces an exception to the general statement in verse 2
- الَّذِينَ is a relative pronoun introducing a relative clause describing the excepted group
- The attached pronoun وا (waw al-jama'ah) on the verbs indicates 3rd person masculine plural
- Form VI verb تَوَاصَوْا indicates mutual/reciprocal action (they counseled each other)
- Both الصَّالِحَاتِ (feminine plural) and الْحَقِّ/الصَّبْرِ show definite article usage
- The repeated وَ creates parallel structure linking four conditions of salvation

## Grammar Summary
- **Oath construction**: وَالْعَصْرِ demonstrates how oath particles govern the genitive case
- **إِنَّ structure**: Emphasis particle that makes the subject accusative and requires a predicate (khabar)
- **Exception with إِلَّا**: Shows how to exclude a group from a general statement
- **Relative clauses**: الَّذِينَ introduces clauses that describe who is being referred to
- **Verb conjugation**: Past tense plural verbs with waw al-jama'ah suffix (وا-)
- **Form VI verbs**: تَوَاصَوْا demonstrates mutual/reciprocal action pattern
- **Prepositional phrases**: بِالْحَقِّ and بِالصَّبْرِ show how prepositions govern the genitive case
- **Parallel structure**: Repeated وَ coordinates multiple verbs creating rhythmic balance
