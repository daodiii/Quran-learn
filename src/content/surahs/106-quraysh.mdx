# Quraysh (Quraysh)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 4
- **Theme:** Reminds the Quraysh tribe of Allah's blessings - their safe trade journeys and the security of Mecca - and calls them to worship the Lord of the Ka'bah.
- **Grammar Focus:** لِإِيلَافِ (li-ilafi) construction expressing causation, idafa (possessive constructions), imperative mood verbs, relative clauses with الَّذِي (alladhi)

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** لِإِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ

**Translation:** "For the accustoming of Quraysh"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | لِ | li | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition (for causation/purpose) - not declinable | for, because of |
| 2 | إِيلَافِ | ilafi | ا ل ف | Noun - verbal noun (masdar), masculine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | accustoming, familiarizing |
| 3 | قُرَيْشٍ | quraysh | - | Proper noun - indefinite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with tanwin kasra | Quraysh (tribe name) |

#### Grammar Notes
- لِ here expresses causation or purpose, linking to the previous surah (Al-Fil)
- إِيلَافِ is a masdar (verbal noun) from Form IV verb آلَفَ (to make familiar/accustomed)
- إِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ is an idafa construction: "the accustoming of Quraysh"
- Some scholars connect this verse's لِ to the final verb of Surah Al-Fil (فَجَعَلَهُمْ)
- The meaning: Because of Quraysh's established custom (of safe trade)...

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** إِيلَافِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ

**Translation:** "Their accustoming to the journey of winter and summer"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | إِيلَافِهِمْ | ilafihim | ا ل ف | Noun - verbal noun (masdar), masculine, singular, definite + attached pronoun | Apposition/clarification of previous إِيلَافِ - genitive (majrur), + possessive pronoun genitive | their accustoming |
| 2 | رِحْلَةَ | rihlata | ر ح ل | Noun - feminine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Object of the verbal noun - accusative (mansub) with fatha | journey, caravan |
| 3 | الشِّتَاءِ | ash-shita'i | ش ت و | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with kasra | the winter |
| 4 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 5 | الصَّيْفِ | as-sayfi | ص ي ف | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Coordinate with ash-shita'i - genitive (majrur) with kasra | the summer |

#### Grammar Notes
- إِيلَافِهِمْ clarifies/repeats the previous إِيلَافِ with added pronoun هِمْ (their)
- رِحْلَةَ is the object of the verbal noun إِيلَافِ (what they were accustomed to)
- رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ is an idafa: "the journey of winter and summer"
- الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ: coordinated nouns both in genitive (majrur)
- Historical context: Quraysh had two annual trade caravans (winter to Yemen, summer to Syria)

### Verse 3

**Arabic:** فَلْيَعْبُدُوا رَبَّ هَـٰذَا الْبَيْتِ

**Translation:** "Let them worship the Lord of this House"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | فَ | fa | - | Particle - conjunction | Consequential conjunction - not declinable | so, then |
| 2 | لْ | li | - | Particle - command | Imperative particle (lam al-amr) - not declinable | let (command) |
| 3 | يَعْبُدُوا | ya'budu | ع ب د | Verb - Form I, present tense (jussive), 3rd person, masculine, plural | Jussive verb (fi'l mudari' majzum) after lam al-amr - jussive with dropped nun | they worship |
| 4 | رَبَّ | rabba | ر ب ب | Noun - masculine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with fatha | Lord |
| 5 | هَـٰذَا | hadha | - | Demonstrative pronoun - near, masculine singular | First part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive position | this |
| 6 | الْبَيْتِ | al-bayti | ب ي ت | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with kasra | the House |

#### Grammar Notes
- فَ connects this command as the logical consequence of verses 1-2
- لْ (lam al-amr) is the imperative particle for third person commands
- يَعْبُدُوا is jussive after lam al-amr (original would be يَعْبُدُونَ, nun is dropped)
- رَبَّ هَـٰذَا الْبَيْتِ forms an idafa chain: "Lord of this House"
- هَـٰذَا الْبَيْتِ: demonstrative + noun = "this House" (the Ka'bah)
- The verse's logic: Since Allah protected your commerce, worship Him

### Verse 4

**Arabic:** الَّذِي أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ وَآمَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍ

**Translation:** "Who has fed them against hunger and made them safe from fear"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّذِي | alladhi | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine singular | Adjective describing rabba - accusative (mansub) position | Who, the One who |
| 2 | أَطْعَمَهُم | at'amahum | ط ع م | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) + object pronoun accusative | fed them |
| 3 | مِّن | min | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | from, against |
| 4 | جُوعٍ | ju'in | ج و ع | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | hunger |
| 5 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 6 | آمَنَهُم | amanahum | ا م ن | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) + object pronoun accusative | made them safe |
| 7 | مِّنْ | min | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | from |
| 8 | خَوْفٍ | khawfin | خ و ف | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | fear |

#### Grammar Notes
- الَّذِي is a relative pronoun describing رَبَّ (the Lord)
- أَطْعَمَ is Form IV (causative: "to cause to eat" = "to feed")
- أَطْعَمَهُم: verb + object pronoun هُم (them)
- مِّن here means "from/against" (protection from something)
- آمَنَ is Form IV from ا م ن (to make safe/secure)
- The parallel structure: أَطْعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ / آمَنَهُم مِّنْ خَوْفٍ
- Two divine blessings: provision (food) and security (safety)

## Grammar Summary
- **لِ for causation**: لِإِيلَافِ expresses reason/purpose, often linking thoughts across surahs
- **Idafa constructions**: إِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ, رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ, رَبَّ هَـٰذَا الْبَيْتِ showing possessive/descriptive relationships
- **Verbal noun (masdar)**: إِيلَافِ as object of preposition with its own object رِحْلَةَ
- **Imperative with لْ**: فَلْيَعْبُدُوا commanding third person (let them worship)
- **Jussive mood**: يَعْبُدُوا with dropped nun showing jussive after lam al-amr
- **Demonstrative + noun**: هَـٰذَا الْبَيْتِ (this House) referring to the Ka'bah
- **Relative clause**: الَّذِي introduces description of the Lord
- **Form IV causative verbs**: أَطْعَمَ (caused to eat/fed), آمَنَ (made safe)
- **Attached pronouns**: ـهِمْ (their/them) showing possession and objects
- **مِّن expressing protection**: مِّن جُوعٍ, مِّنْ خَوْفٍ (from/against hunger and fear)
- **Parallel structure**: Two coordinated verbal phrases with balanced grammar for rhetorical effect
- **Consequential فَ**: فَلْيَعْبُدُوا showing logical conclusion
