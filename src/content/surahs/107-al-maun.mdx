# Al-Maʿun (Small Kindnesses)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 7
- **Theme:** Condemns religious hypocrisy, describing those who deny the Day of Judgment, mistreat orphans, neglect the poor, and perform prayers ostentatiously while neglecting small acts of kindness.
- **Grammar Focus:** Interrogative أَرَأَيْتَ (ara'ayta), relative clauses with الَّذِي/الَّذِينَ (alladhi/alladhina), nominal sentences with فَـ (fa), present tense verbs, active participles

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّينِ

**Translation:** "Have you seen the one who denies the Recompense?"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | أَ | a | - | Particle - interrogative | Interrogative particle - not declinable | question marker |
| 2 | رَأَيْتَ | ra'ayta | ر ا ي | Verb - Form I, past tense, 2nd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | you saw, you have seen |
| 3 | الَّذِي | alladhi | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine singular | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) position | the one who, he who |
| 4 | يُكَذِّبُ | yukadhdhibu | ك ذ ب | Verb - Form II, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') | denies, rejects |
| 5 | بِ | bi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | regarding, with |
| 6 | الدِّينِ | ad-din | د ي ن | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | the religion, the Judgment |

#### Grammar Notes
- أَرَأَيْتَ is a rhetorical question formula (Have you seen/considered?)
- الَّذِي is the direct object of رَأَيْتَ, introducing a relative clause
- يُكَذِّبُ is Form II (intensive/declarative: "to call someone a liar" or "to deny")
- الدِّينِ here refers to the Day of Judgment and divine recompense
- The interrogative أَ draws attention to something remarkable or condemnable

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** فَذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي يَدُعُّ الْيَتِيمَ

**Translation:** "For that is the one who repels the orphan"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | فَ | fa | - | Particle - conjunction | Consequential/explanatory conjunction - not declinable | so, for, then |
| 2 | ذَٰلِكَ | dhalika | - | Demonstrative pronoun - far, masculine singular | Subject (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') | that, that one |
| 3 | الَّذِي | alladhi | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine singular | Predicate (khabar) - nominative (marfu') | the one who |
| 4 | يَدُعُّ | yadu''u | د ع ع | Verb - Form I, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') | pushes away, repels |
| 5 | الْيَتِيمَ | al-yatima | ي ت م | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with fatha | the orphan |

#### Grammar Notes
- فَ here is explanatory/consequential (so, for that reason)
- ذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي forms a nominal sentence: "That one is the one who..."
- ذَٰلِكَ refers back to الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّينِ in verse 1
- يَدُعُّ has doubled ع showing emphasis in pronunciation
- The present tense يَدُعُّ indicates habitual/characteristic action
- This verse identifies the denier by his cruel treatment of orphans

### Verse 3

**Arabic:** وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ الْمِسْكِينِ

**Translation:** "And does not encourage the feeding of the poor"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 2 | لَا | la | - | Particle - negation | Negation particle - not declinable | not |
| 3 | يَحُضُّ | yahuddu | ح ض ض | Verb - Form I, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') | encourages, urges |
| 4 | عَلَىٰ | 'ala | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | upon, to |
| 5 | طَعَامِ | ta'ami | ط ع م | Noun - masculine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | food, feeding |
| 6 | الْمِسْكِينِ | al-miskini | س ك ن | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with kasra | the poor person |

#### Grammar Notes
- وَ coordinates this verb with the previous يَدُعُّ (two characteristics)
- لَا negates present tense verbs without changing their mood (still marfu')
- يَحُضُّ has doubled ض for emphasis
- طَعَامِ الْمِسْكِينِ is an idafa: "the food of the poor" = "feeding the poor"
- This verse adds another dimension: not only does he harm (orphans), he doesn't help (the poor)

### Verse 4

**Arabic:** فَوَيْلٌ لِّلْمُصَلِّينَ

**Translation:** "So woe to those who pray"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | فَ | fa | - | Particle - conjunction | Consequential conjunction - not declinable | so, then |
| 2 | وَيْلٌ | waylun | و ي ل | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Subject (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') with tanwin damma | woe, destruction |
| 3 | لِّ | li | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | for, to |
| 4 | لْمُصَلِّينَ | al-musallin | ص ل و | Noun - active participle, masculine, plural, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with ya (sound masculine plural ending) | those who pray, the prayers |

#### Grammar Notes
- فَ connects this as consequence of the described behavior
- وَيْلٌ لِّ is the exclamatory construction seen in Surah 104
- الْمُصَلِّينَ is an active participle (ism al-fa'il) used as a noun
- الْمُصَلِّينَ is in the genitive plural (ending with ـينَ in genitive/accusative)
- This seems surprising (woe to prayers?) but verses 5-7 qualify which prayers are meant

### Verse 5

**Arabic:** الَّذِينَ هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ

**Translation:** "Who are heedless of their prayer"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّذِينَ | alladhina | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine plural | Adjective describing al-musallin - genitive (majrur) | those who, they who |
| 2 | هُمْ | hum | - | Pronoun - detached, 3rd person masculine plural | Subject of nominal sentence (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') | they |
| 3 | عَن | 'an | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | from, about, regarding |
| 4 | صَلَاتِهِمْ | salatihim | ص ل و | Noun - feminine, singular, definite + attached pronoun | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra, + possessive pronoun genitive | their prayer |
| 5 | سَاهُونَ | sahuna | س ه و | Noun - active participle, masculine, plural, indefinite | Predicate (khabar) - nominative (marfu') with waw + nun (sound masculine plural) | heedless, forgetful |

#### Grammar Notes
- الَّذِينَ introduces a relative clause describing which prayers
- هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ is a nominal sentence: "they are heedless of their prayer"
- عَن here means "regarding/concerning" (heedless about)
- سَاهُونَ is an active participle (ism al-fa'il) functioning as predicate
- The meaning: those who pray but without attention or sincerity

### Verse 6

**Arabic:** الَّذِينَ هُمْ يُرَاءُونَ

**Translation:** "Those who make show [of their deeds]"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّذِينَ | alladhina | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine plural | Adjective/apposition describing previous الَّذِينَ - genitive (majrur) | those who |
| 2 | هُمْ | hum | - | Pronoun - detached, 3rd person masculine plural | Subject of nominal sentence (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') | they |
| 3 | يُرَاءُونَ | yura'una | ر ا ي | Verb - Form III, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate (khabar) - indicative (marfu') with waw + nun | show off, act hypocritically |

#### Grammar Notes
- الَّذِينَ is either a second relative clause or apposition to the previous one
- هُمْ يُرَاءُونَ is a nominal sentence with verbal predicate
- يُرَاءُونَ is Form III from ر ا ي, meaning "to show off" or "to act for people to see"
- The verb يُرَاءُونَ indicates doing acts of worship for show, not sincerity (riya')
- This adds another characteristic: their prayers are for public display

### Verse 7

**Arabic:** وَيَمْنَعُونَ الْمَاعُونَ

**Translation:** "And withhold [simple] assistance"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 2 | يَمْنَعُونَ | yamna'una | م ن ع | Verb - Form I, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') with waw + nun | withhold, prevent, refuse |
| 3 | الْمَاعُونَ | al-ma'un | م ع ن | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with fatha | small kindnesses, simple assistance |

#### Grammar Notes
- وَ coordinates this final characteristic with the previous verbs
- يَمْنَعُونَ is present tense, plural, indicative
- الْمَاعُونَ refers to small acts of kindness or simple tools/assistance people need
- The surah's name comes from this word
- Final irony: they pray publicly but refuse the smallest help to neighbors
- The accusative الْمَاعُونَ (with fatha) shows it's the direct object of يَمْنَعُونَ

## Grammar Summary
- **Rhetorical interrogative**: أَرَأَيْتَ drawing attention to something noteworthy
- **Relative pronouns**: الَّذِي (singular), الَّذِينَ (plural) introducing descriptive clauses
- **Demonstrative pronouns**: ذَٰلِكَ pointing to someone/something
- **Nominal sentences**: ذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي, هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ, هُمْ يُرَاءُونَ
- **Consequential/explanatory فَ**: فَذَٰلِكَ, فَوَيْلٌ showing logical connection
- **Present tense verbs**: يُكَذِّبُ, يَدُعُّ, يَحُضُّ, يُرَاءُونَ, يَمْنَعُونَ indicating habitual action
- **Negation with لَا**: لَا يَحُضُّ negating present tense without mood change
- **Form II intensive verbs**: يُكَذِّبُ with doubled middle letter
- **Form III verbs**: يُرَاءُونَ indicating interaction/mutual action (showing off to others)
- **Active participles**: الْمُصَلِّينَ, سَاهُونَ functioning as nouns/adjectives
- **Idafa construction**: طَعَامِ الْمِسْكِينِ (feeding of the poor)
- **Exclamatory وَيْلٌ construction**: فَوَيْلٌ لِّلْمُصَلِّينَ expressing condemnation
- **Prepositional phrases**: بِالدِّينِ, عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ
- **Attached pronouns**: ـهِمْ showing possession
- **Sound plural endings**: الْمُصَلِّينَ (ـينَ genitive), سَاهُونَ (ـونَ nominative)
