# Al-Humazah (The Slanderer)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 9
- **Theme:** A stern warning against those who slander others and accumulate wealth, describing their punishment in hellfire.
- **Grammar Focus:** Exclamatory construction with وَيْلٌ (waylun), relative clauses, past tense verbs, nominal sentences, interrogative أَدْرَاكَ (adraka), demonstrative pronouns

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** وَيْلٌ لِّكُلِّ هُمَزَةٍ لُّمَزَةٍ

**Translation:** "Woe to every scorner and mocker"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَيْلٌ | waylun | و ي ل | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Subject (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') with tanwin damma | woe, destruction |
| 2 | لِّ | li | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition with assimilation - not declinable | for, to |
| 3 | كُلِّ | kulli | ك ل ل | Noun - masculine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | every |
| 4 | هُمَزَةٍ | humazatin | ه م ز | Noun - masculine/feminine, singular, indefinite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with tanwin kasra | scorner, slanderer |
| 5 | لُّمَزَةٍ | lumazatin | ل م ز | Noun - masculine/feminine, singular, indefinite | Adjective/apposition to humazatin - genitive with tanwin kasra | mocker, fault-finder |

#### Grammar Notes
- وَيْلٌ is an exclamation expressing threat or curse
- كُلِّ is made definite through idafa (possessive construction) with هُمَزَةٍ
- Both هُمَزَةٍ and لُّمَزَةٍ follow the فُعَلَة pattern indicating exaggerated/habitual action
- The assimilated lam (لِّ) shows how prepositions can merge with following letters

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** الَّذِي جَمَعَ مَالًا وَعَدَّدَهُ

**Translation:** "Who collects wealth and counts it"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّذِي | alladhi | - | Pronoun - relative, masculine singular | Adjective/attribute describing humazatin - genitive (majrur) | who, that which |
| 2 | جَمَعَ | jama'a | ج م ع | Verb - Form I, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l madi) - indicative | collected, gathered |
| 3 | مَالًا | malan | م و ل | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Direct object (maf'ul bihi) - accusative (mansub) with tanwin fatha | wealth, property |
| 4 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 5 | عَدَّدَهُ | 'addadahu | ع د د | Verb - Form II, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Verb with attached object pronoun - verb indicative, pronoun accusative (mansub) | counted it |

#### Grammar Notes
- الَّذِي is a relative pronoun describing the type of person mentioned in verse 1
- Form II verb عَدَّدَ (with doubled middle letter) indicates intensity or repetition
- The attached pronoun هُ refers back to مَالًا (his wealth)
- This verse provides the defining characteristic of the person being condemned

### Verse 3

**Arabic:** يَحْسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُ أَخْلَدَهُ

**Translation:** "He thinks that his wealth will make him immortal"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | يَحْسَبُ | yahsabu | ح س ب | Verb - Form I, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') | thinks, supposes |
| 2 | أَنَّ | anna | - | Particle - emphasis | Subordinating particle (harf tawkid) - governs accusative | that |
| 3 | مَالَهُ | malahu | م و ل | Noun - masculine, singular, definite + attached pronoun | Subject of anna (ism anna) - accusative (mansub) with fatha, + possessive pronoun genitive | his wealth |
| 4 | أَخْلَدَهُ | akhladahu | خ ل د | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Predicate of anna (khabar anna) - nominative (marfu') position, + object pronoun accusative | made him immortal |

#### Grammar Notes
- يَحْسَبُ is a verb of supposition that takes a sentential object introduced by أَنَّ
- أَنَّ is similar to إِنَّ but used in subordinate clauses
- The subject-predicate order in the أَنَّ clause: مَالَهُ (subject) أَخْلَدَهُ (predicate)
- Form IV أَخْلَدَ means "to make eternal/immortal"

### Verse 4

**Arabic:** كَلَّا ۖ لَيُنبَذَنَّ فِي الْحُطَمَةِ

**Translation:** "No! He will surely be thrown into the Crusher"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | كَلَّا | kalla | - | Particle - negation/rebuke | Negation particle - not declinable | No! Nay! |
| 2 | لَ | la | - | Particle - emphasis | Emphasis prefix (lam al-tawkid) - not declinable | surely |
| 3 | يُنبَذَنَّ | yunbadhanna | ن ب ذ | Verb - Form I passive, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + nun al-tawkid | Passive verb (fi'l majhul mudari') with emphatic nun - indicative | will be thrown |
| 4 | فِي | fi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | in, into |
| 5 | الْحُطَمَةِ | al-hutamah | ح ط م | Noun - feminine, singular, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | the Crusher |

#### Grammar Notes
- كَلَّا is a strong negation/rejection particle
- The لَ prefix adds oath-like emphasis to the verb
- يُنبَذَنَّ is passive voice (the doer is unspecified, focus on the action done to him)
- The emphatic nun (نَّ) suffix reinforces certainty
- الْحُطَمَةِ is a name for hellfire, derived from the root meaning "to crush"

### Verse 5

**Arabic:** وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْحُطَمَةُ

**Translation:** "And what can make you know what is the Crusher?"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَ | wa | - | Particle - conjunction | Coordinating conjunction - not declinable | and |
| 2 | مَا | ma | - | Pronoun - interrogative | Interrogative pronoun - nominative (marfu') | what |
| 3 | أَدْرَاكَ | adraka | د ر ك | Verb - Form IV, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, singular + attached pronoun | Verb with attached object pronoun - verb indicative, pronoun accusative | made you know, informed you |
| 4 | مَا | ma | - | Pronoun - interrogative | Subject (mubtada) - nominative (marfu') | what |
| 5 | الْحُطَمَةُ | al-hutamah | ح ط م | Noun - feminine, singular, definite | Predicate (khabar) - nominative (marfu') with damma | the Crusher |

#### Grammar Notes
- وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ is a rhetorical question formula emphasizing something momentous
- The first مَا is interrogative, the second begins the nominal sentence
- أَدْرَاكَ (Form IV) means "to cause to know/perceive"
- The ـكَ suffix is the 2nd person singular object pronoun (you)
- This formula is common in the Quran to introduce something significant

### Verse 6

**Arabic:** نَارُ اللَّهِ الْمُوقَدَةُ

**Translation:** "It is the fire of Allah, kindled"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | نَارُ | naru | ن و ر | Noun - feminine, singular, definite (by idafa) | Subject/predicate (mubtada/khabar) - nominative (marfu') with damma | fire |
| 2 | اللَّهِ | allah | - | Proper noun - definite | Second part of idafa (mudaf ilayh) - genitive with kasra | Allah, God |
| 3 | الْمُوقَدَةُ | al-muqadah | و ق د | Noun - active participle, feminine, singular, definite | Adjective describing naru - nominative (marfu') with damma | the kindled one |

#### Grammar Notes
- This verse is the answer to the rhetorical question in verse 5
- نَارُ اللَّهِ forms an idafa (possessive construction): "the fire of Allah"
- الْمُوقَدَةُ is an active participle functioning as an adjective
- The feminine form الْمُوقَدَةُ agrees with the feminine noun نَارُ

### Verse 7

**Arabic:** الَّتِي تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى الْأَفْئِدَةِ

**Translation:** "Which mounts directed at the hearts"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّتِي | allati | - | Pronoun - relative, feminine singular | Adjective describing al-muqadah - nominative (marfu') | which, that which |
| 2 | تَطَّلِعُ | tattali'u | ط ل ع | Verb - Form V, present tense, 3rd person, feminine, singular | Predicate verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu') | rises up, mounts |
| 3 | عَلَى | 'ala | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | upon, over, at |
| 4 | الْأَفْئِدَةِ | al-af'idah | ف ا د | Noun - feminine, plural, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with kasra | the hearts |

#### Grammar Notes
- الَّتِي is the feminine singular form of the relative pronoun
- تَطَّلِعُ (Form V) shows the doubled ط from assimilation (originally تَتَطَلَّعُ)
- عَلَى governs the genitive case on الْأَفْئِدَةِ
- الْأَفْئِدَةِ is the plural of فُؤَاد (heart), following the أَفْعِلَة pattern

### Verse 8

**Arabic:** إِنَّهَا عَلَيْهِم مُّؤْصَدَةٌ

**Translation:** "Indeed, it will be closed down upon them"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | إِنَّهَا | innaha | - | Particle + attached pronoun | Emphasis particle (harf tawkid) + subject pronoun (ism inna) accusative | indeed it |
| 2 | عَلَيْهِم | 'alayhim | - | Preposition + attached pronoun | Preposition + pronoun genitive | upon them |
| 3 | مُّؤْصَدَةٌ | mu'sadatun | ا ص د | Noun - passive participle, feminine, singular, indefinite | Predicate of inna (khabar inna) - nominative (marfu') with tanwin damma | closed, locked |

#### Grammar Notes
- إِنَّ with attached pronoun هَا (it, referring to the fire)
- عَلَيْهِم is a prepositional phrase with attached pronoun هِم (them)
- مُّؤْصَدَةٌ is a passive participle (ism maf'ul) meaning "closed/locked"
- The predicate position allows for an indefinite noun despite إِنَّ structure

### Verse 9

**Arabic:** فِي عَمَدٍ مُّمَدَّدَةٍ

**Translation:** "In columns extended"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | فِي | fi | - | Particle - preposition | Preposition - not declinable | in, by |
| 2 | عَمَدٍ | 'amadin | ع م د | Noun - feminine, plural, indefinite | Object of preposition (majrur) - genitive with tanwin kasra | columns, pillars |
| 3 | مُّمَدَّدَةٍ | mumaddadatin | م د د | Noun - passive participle, feminine, plural, indefinite | Adjective describing 'amadin - genitive with tanwin kasra | extended, stretched |

#### Grammar Notes
- This verse describes how the fire is enclosed with extended columns/pillars
- عَمَدٍ is plural of عَمُود (column, pillar)
- مُّمَدَّدَةٍ is a passive participle from Form II verb مَدَّدَ (to extend, stretch)
- Both عَمَدٍ and مُّمَدَّدَةٍ are genitive due to the preposition فِي
- The image depicts an inescapable prison of fire

## Grammar Summary
- **Exclamatory وَيْلٌ construction**: Used to express curse or condemnation
- **Idafa (possessive) constructions**: كُلِّ هُمَزَةٍ, نَارُ اللَّهِ showing noun-noun relationships
- **Relative clauses**: الَّذِي and الَّتِي introducing descriptive clauses
- **Emphatic particles**: أَنَّ/إِنَّ governing accusative case on the subject
- **Attached pronouns**: ـهُ, ـكَ, ـهَا, ـهِم showing possessive and object relationships
- **Passive voice**: يُنبَذَ showing action done to subject with unspecified doer
- **Participial forms**: الْمُوقَدَةُ (active), مُّؤْصَدَةٌ (passive) functioning as adjectives
- **Rhetorical interrogative**: وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ formula for emphasis
- **Form II verbs**: عَدَّدَ showing intensive/repetitive action
- **Form IV verbs**: أَخْلَدَ showing causative meaning
- **Form V verbs**: تَطَّلِعُ with characteristic ta- prefix
