# Al-Mutaffifin (The Defrauders)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 36
- **Theme:** Condemnation of fraud in weights and measures, contrasting the fate of the wicked (fujjar) who deny the Day of Judgment with the righteous (abrar) who will enjoy Paradise.
- **Grammar Focus:** Exclamatory constructions (waylun), rhetorical questions (a-la), emphasis particles (inna, kalla), described noun patterns (kitabun marqum), idafa constructions, contrast structures, temporal adverbs (yawma'idhin), active participles as substantives.

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** وَيْلٌ لِّلْمُطَفِّفِينَ

**Translation:** "Woe to those who give less [than due]."

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | وَيْلٌ | waylun | و ي ل | Noun - masculine, singular, indefinite | Subject (mubtada') - nominative (marfu') with damma tanwin | Woe/destruction |
| 2 | لِّلْمُطَفِّفِينَ | lil-mutaffifina | ط ف ف | Particle + Noun - active participle Form II, masculine, plural, definite | li = preposition governing genitive; al-mutaffifina = object of preposition (majrur) with kasra | For the defrauders/those who give less |

#### Grammar Notes
- Exclamatory nominal sentence with predicate omitted (khabr mahdhuf), implying "destruction is for them"
- Active participle mutaffifin (Form II) from root t-f-f (to cheat in measurement)

### Verse 2

**Arabic:** الَّذِينَ إِذَا اكْتَالُوا عَلَى النَّاسِ يَسْتَوْفُونَ

**Translation:** "Who, when they take a measure from people, take in full."

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | الَّذِينَ | alladhina | - | Relative pronoun - masculine plural | Adjective (na't) describing al-mutaffifina - follows case (majrur) | Those who |
| 2 | إِذَا | idha | - | Particle - temporal conditional | Not declinable (mabni); introduces temporal clause | When |
| 3 | اكْتَالُوا | iktalu | ك ي ل | Verb - Form VIII, past tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Past verb in temporal clause; waw = subject pronoun (fa'il) | They take/receive measure |
| 4 | عَلَى | 'ala | - | Particle - preposition | Not declinable (mabni); governs genitive | From/upon |
| 5 | النَّاسِ | al-nasi | ن و س | Noun - masculine, plural, definite | Object of preposition (majrur) with kasra | The people |
| 6 | يَسْتَوْفُونَ | yastawfuna | و ف ي | Verb - Form X, present tense, 3rd person, masculine, plural | Present verb (fi'l mudari') - indicative (marfu'); waw = subject pronoun | They take in full |

#### Grammar Notes
- Relative clause (sila) defining mutaffifin
- idha temporal conditional structure
- Form VIII iktalu means "to receive measure for oneself"
- Form X yastawfuna means "to demand/take the full amount"

- Form II thuwwiba (passive): were they not fully rewarded/requited?
- ma kanu yaf'alun: relative clause (what they used to do)
- kana + mudari' for habitual past action
- Closing emphasizes divine justice: perfect recompense

## Grammar Summary

- **Exclamatory constructions:** waylun + li (verses 1, 10) - nominal sentence with omitted predicate
- **Emphasis particles:** kalla (negation/transition), inna (assertion), la (emphasis with inna)
- **Rhetorical questions:** a-la yazunnu (verse 4), ma adraka (verses 8, 19), hal thuwwiba (verse 36)
- **Described noun patterns:** kitabun marqumun (verses 9, 20)
- **Idafa constructions:** kitab al-fujjar/al-abrar, rabb al-'alamin, yawm al-din, nadrat al-na'im
- **Temporal adverbs:** yawma'idhin (compound: that Day), al-yawma (today - Day of Judgment)
- **Contrast structures:** kalla...inna pattern marking transitions between fujjar and abrar (verses 7, 14, 18)
- **Active/passive participles as substantives:** mutaffifin, mukadhdhibin, muqarrabun, dallun
- **Form patterns:** Form II (intensive: yukadhdhibun, thuwwiba), Form IV (causative: yukhsirun, adraka), Form VI (reciprocal: yataghamazun), Form VII (reflexive: inqalabu), Form VIII (reflexive: iktalu), Form X (seeking: yastawfun)
- **Conditional structures:** idha + past verb + present verb (verses 2, 3, 30, 31, 32)
- **kana + mudari' construction:** Habitual past action (verses 14, 29, 36)
- **Passive voice:** tutla, yuqalu, ursilu, yusqawn, thuwwiba
- **Hal circumstantial qualifier:** hafizina, fakihina (state descriptions)
- **Prepositional phrases as predicates:** la-fi sijjin, la-fi 'illiyyin, la-fi na'im
