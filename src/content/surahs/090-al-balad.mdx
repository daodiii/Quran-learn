# Al-Balad (The City)

## Overview

- **Revelation:** Meccan
- **Verses:** 20
- **Theme:** This surah begins with an oath by the sacred city of Makkah, then discusses the struggles and tests of human life. It criticizes those who squander wealth arrogantly and emphasizes the importance of freeing slaves, feeding the poor, and having faith. The righteous are described as people of the right hand, while the disbelievers are people of the left.
- **Grammar Focus:** Oath constructions with لَا أُقْسِمُ (la uqsimu), conditional particles, relative clauses with ism mawsul (الَّذِي/الَّذِينَ), past tense narrative, nominal sentences, emphasis with هَلْ

## Verse-by-Verse Analysis

### Verse 1

**Arabic:** لَا أُقْسِمُ بِهَٰذَا الْبَلَدِ

**Translation:** "I swear by this city"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | لَا | la | - | Particle - negation/extra | Not declinable (mabni), emphatic particle | not (emphatic) |
| 2 | أُقْسِمُ | uqsimu | ق-س-م | Verb - Form IV, present, 1st person singular | Verb in indicative (marfu') | I swear |
| 3 | بِ | bi- | - | Particle - preposition | Not declinable (mabni), governs genitive | by |
| 4 | هَٰذَا | hadha | - | Demonstrative - near, masculine, singular | Not declinable (mabni) in place of genitive after بِ | this |
| 5 | الْبَلَدِ | al-baladi | ب-ل-د | Noun - masculine, singular, definite | Apposition (badal) to هَٰذَا - genitive (majrur) | the city |

#### Grammar Notes
- لَا أُقْسِمُ is emphatic oath formula (لَا is extra, not negating)
- هَٰذَا الْبَلَدِ refers to Makkah, the sacred city
- بِ is the oath preposition governing genitive

| 1 | أُولَٰئِكَ | ula'ika | - | Demonstrative - far, masculine, plural | Not declinable (mabni), subject of nominal sentence | those |

#### Grammar Notes
- أُولَٰئِكَ refers to those who broke through the steep pass (verses 13-17)
- أَصْحَابُ الْمَيْمَنَةِ: "people of the right [hand]" - the blessed/saved
- الْمَيْمَنَةِ from ي-م-ن (right, blessing) - maf'ala pattern for place

### Verse 19

**Arabic:** وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بِآيَاتِنَا هُمْ أَصْحَابُ الْمَشْأَمَةِ

**Translation:** "But those who disbelieved in Our signs - those are the companions of the left"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|

### Verse 20

**Arabic:** عَلَيْهِمْ نَارٌ مُّؤْصَدَةٌ

**Translation:** "Over them will be fire closed in"

#### Word-by-Word Breakdown

| # | Arabic | Transliteration | Root | Morphology | I'rab | Meaning |
|---|--------|----------------|------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1 | عَلَيْهِمْ | 'alayhim | - | Preposition + attached pronoun | عَلَى governs genitive, هِمْ 3rd person plural pronoun | over/upon them |
| 2 | نَارٌ | narun | ن-و-ر | Noun - feminine, singular, indefinite | Subject (mubtada') - nominative (marfu'), with tanwin dammatayn | fire |
| 3 | مُّؤْصَدَةٌ | mu'sadatun | أ-ص-د | Noun - feminine, singular, indefinite (passive participle Form IV) | Adjective (na't) of نَارٌ - nominative (marfu'), with tanwin dammatayn | closed/sealed |

#### Grammar Notes
- Nominal sentence with prepositional phrase first for emphasis
- نَارٌ is the subject (mubtada'), delayed after the prepositional phrase
- مُّؤْصَدَةٌ Form IV passive participle: "closed, locked, sealed"
- Describes eternal confinement in Hellfire for people of the left

## Grammar Summary

- **Oath constructions**: لَا أُقْسِمُ بِ (verse 1) - emphatic oath formula with لَا for emphasis, وَ oath particle (verse 3)
- **Nominal sentences**: أَنتَ حِلٌّ (verse 2), أُولَٰئِكَ أَصْحَابُ (verse 18), عَلَيْهِمْ نَارٌ (verse 20)
- **Relative clauses with ism mawsul**: الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا (verse 17), الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا (verse 19)
- **Rhetorical questions**: أَيَحْسَبُ (verses 5, 7), أَلَمْ نَجْعَل (verse 8), وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ (verse 12)
- **Dual forms**: عَيْنَيْنِ, شَفَتَيْنِ, النَّجْدَيْنِ - demonstrating dual accusative ي + ن with tanwin
- **The five nouns (الأسماء الخمسة)**: ذِي (genitive), ذَا (accusative) - showing case by long vowels
- **Form IV verbs**: أُقْسِمُ, أَهْلَكْتُ, آمَنُوا
- **Form VI verbs**: تَوَاصَوْا (mutual/reciprocal action)
- **Form VIII verbs**: اقْتَحَمَ (reflexive/intensive)
- **Verbal nouns (masdar)**: فَكُّ, إِطْعَامٌ - used as predicates
- **Idafa constructions**: فَكُّ رَقَبَةٍ, أَصْحَابُ الْمَيْمَنَةِ, أَصْحَابُ الْمَشْأَمَةِ
- **Coordinated structures**: وَ...وَ for listing (verses 9, 17)
- **Emphasis particles**: لَقَدْ (verse 4), هُمْ as pronoun of separation (verse 19)
